Spicy Chicken Curry

Description

A flavorful dish made with tender chicken simmered in a rich, aromatic blend of spices, tomatoes, and coconut milk. It's a hearty, warming meal that's perfect when paired with rice or naan for a satisfying dinner

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 spring onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tbsp ground ginger
  • 200ml chopped tomatoes
  • Chicken thigh or breast
  • 60g rice
  • 200ml water
  • 1 tbsp yogurt
  • Fresh coriander

Steps

  1. Start by heating some oil in a pan and add the chopped spring onions and garlic. Let it cook for 5 minutes then throw in the curry powder and ground ginger. Pour the chopped tomatoes into the pan and mix everything up before adding the chicken. Let it cook for about 5 minutes or until it looks cooked on all sides.
  2. Add a splash of water and a scoop of yogurt, before stirring it together and letting it simmer for another 5 - 10 minutes.
  3. While the curry is simmering, find another pan and cook the rice following the instructions on the packet.
  4. Once everything is cooked, grab a plate and dig in!
